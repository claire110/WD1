Who We Are

Aussie Kidz Charity started in early 2000

We assists the families of children under the age of 18 years old that are diagnosed with a disability

With Aussie Kidz Charity

100% of the donations received are used for the children

keep to an absolute Running costsand overheads minimum and looked after by the Directors and friends.

What We did

raised over $750,000 provided over 150 grants

covering mobility aids, walking frames and other assistance to these very special kids.
